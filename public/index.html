Toronto Metropolitan University, Department of Computer Science

Course: CPS 643 Virtual Reality

Title: Last Stand VR

Authors: Ken Luu, Danny Khuu

Last Stand VR- Survive hordes of enemies with your combat skills in VR

Last Stand VR is a horde survival game in VR, that is packed with waves of enemies, swords, robots, and lasers. Shoot, hack, and slash away through hordes of robots, drones, and mechs to survive as long as possible and become the last one standing. This project aims to create a thrilling, and immersive VR experience by incorporating interesting VR techniques into fun and engaging gameplay.
